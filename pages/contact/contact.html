This app was created using stackblitz.com as final Exam required for the subject Empowerment Technologies for Grade 12 students of La Salle Green Hills. The students were given guidance through different online sources as well as consultations with their amazing teacher through virtual classes.
The users must choose among the six given common activities, input their weight in kilograms (kg), and enter the duration of the activity selected (minutes). Upon entering the necessary information, the user must click the button 'calculate' in order for the system to derive the resulting calories burned and weight lost in kilograms.
